Protein Synthesis Begins

The body starts repairing damaged muscle fibers and initiating the recovery process. This phase begins immediately after your workout and continues for 24-48 hours.

Peak Muscle Protein Synthesis

Recovery reaches its highest point as the body works intensively to repair and build muscle tissue. This typically occurs 24-48 hours after training.

Recovery Complete

Most muscle groups have fully recovered and are ready for the next training session. This typically takes 48-72 hours for most muscle groups, though larger muscle groups may take longer.

Do's and Don'ts
Do This
Train each muscle 2-3x per week
Allow 48-72h between training same muscles
Balance pushing and pulling movements

Don't Do This
Train same muscle groups on consecutive days
Neglect any major muscle groups
Change your program every week
Copy advanced routines as a beginner
